## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide directly
- Account information: name, email address, phone number, password
- Academic information: institution, degree level, field of study, graduation year
- Profile information: interests, career preferences, opportunity preferences
- CV/resume content you create using the CV Builder
- Mentorship information: availability, session notes, messages exchanged with mentors or mentees
- Payment information processed through our payment provider, Paystack (we do not store full card details ourselves)
- Communications you send us, including support requests

### 2.2 Information collected automatically
- Device information (device type, operating system, app version)
- Usage data (features used, pages viewed, session duration)
- Log data (IP address, access times, crash reports)
- Cookies and similar technologies on our website (see Section 8)

### 2.3 Information from third parties
- If you sign in using a third-party service (e.g. Google), we may receive basic profile information from that service
- Scholarship, internship, and fellowship listings may be sourced from public or partner sources

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Match you with relevant opportunities, mentors, and mentorship sessions
- Operate the CV Builder and save your CV content
- Process payments for any paid features
- Send you service-related communications (deadline reminders, digest emails, account notices)
- Send you marketing communications where you have opted in (you may opt out at any time)
- Improve and personalize the Platform
- Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable, we process your personal data on the following bases:
- **Consent** — for optional communications and certain optional features
- **Contract** — to provide the services you've signed up for
- **Legitimate interest** — to improve our Platform and keep it secure
- **Legal obligation** — where required by applicable law

We aim to comply with Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), and, where applicable to users in other jurisdictions, comparable data protection standards.

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with:

- **Service providers** who help us operate the Platform, including:
  - Supabase (database and backend infrastructure)
  - Resend and Zoho Mail (email delivery)
  - Paystack (payment processing)
  - Jitsi (video sessions for mentorship)
- **Mentors or mentees**, limited to the profile and session information necessary to facilitate mentorship
- **Legal authorities**, where required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Voila Africa, our users, or others
- **A successor entity**, in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy or a comparable one
